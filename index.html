Effective date: April 2026

NANOVAC LLC uses Amazon Selling Partner API (SP-API) to retrieve and process order-related information solely for internal order fulfillment operations.

We collect Amazon order information, including shipping address data, only as necessary to fulfill customer orders, generate shipping labels, and ensure accurate delivery.

How We Use Amazon Information

Amazon Information is used exclusively for internal business operations, including order processing, shipping label generation, and fulfillment.

Data Sharing

We do not share Amazon Information with outside parties and use it only for internal order fulfillment operations.

Data Retention

Personally identifiable information is retained only as long as necessary for order fulfillment and operational requirements, and is deleted in accordance with Amazon policies.

Data Security

We use access controls, encrypted storage, secure credentials management, and internal security procedures to protect Amazon Information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.
